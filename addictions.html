Addictions

In the Rooms

In the Rooms is an online recovery tool that connects individuals to virtual meetings dealing with addiction recovery and other similar mental health issues. Meetings are offered for 12 step programs as well as other recovery plans. The site also offers blog posts, informational articles, and information on other treatment programs.

Alcoholics Anonymous

Alcoholics Anonymous is a group dedicated to helping people deal with alcoholism through support and communication with other struggling individuals. The program utilizes anonymous meetings as well as a twelve-step program to help individuals work through their struggles.

Eating Disorders Anonymous

Eating Disorders Anonymous is a group dedicated to helping individuals who have in the past or are currently dealing with an eating disorder. EDA utilizes both in person and virtual meetings as well as a twelve-step program to allow individuals to gain support from others who are experiencing similar struggles and work through their addiction.

Porn Addicts Anonymous

Porn Addicts Anonymous is a group that utilizes anonymous meetings and a twelve-step program to help individuals work through and recover from porn addiction. Members are given the opportunity to communicate with and receive support from other individuals going through similar struggles.
