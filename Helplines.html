Mental Health America Warmline
Mental Health America offers warmlines for those that are not in crisis but are wanting to talk to someone and receive support during difficult times. Warmlines are free confidential support. Alabama warmlines are open 2pm-8pm on Mondays - Fridays and 3pm-8pm.
Call: 844-999-4647
Find A Helpline
Find A Helplines assists in finding and contacting suicide, depression, and anxiety hotlines. Hotlines are free, confidential support with trained peer volunteers over the phone, text, or through online chat.
