On Campus Therapy

UA Counseling Center

The Counseling Center offers individual therapy and support groups. The first session is a free screening to determine counseling needs and subsequent sessions cost $15. They also offer same-day counseling sessions if you call the office number. The Counseling Center is located at 1101 Jackson Avenue. To schedule an appointment, call the office number or go to the counseling center office. You can also contact the Counseling center outside of regular business hours and ask to speak to the on-call counselor.

Office Number: 205-348-3863

UAPD Number (ask for WGRC advocate): 205-348-5454

Women and Gender Resource Center (WGRC)

Women and Gender Resource Center (WGRC) offers individual and group therapy. Services offered for sexual harassment, victimization, and emotional support. Services are offered to all genders and are free. The WGRC is located at 1101 Jackson Avenue. To schedule an appointment, call the office number or go to the WGRC office. You can also contact UAPD outside of regular business hours and ask to speak to an on-call WGRC advocate UAPD.

Office Number: 205-348-5040

UAPD Number (ask for WGRC advocate): 205-348-5454

Capstone Family Therapy Clinic

The Capstone Family Therapy Clinic offers therapy for couples, individuals, and families with a focus on family and social dynamics. Sessions are approximately $5 though there is financial assistance available. The Capstone Family Clinic is located at 214 Child Development, 5th Avenue East. To schedule an appointment, call the office number during their office hours.

Office Number: 205-348-8154

Collegiate Recovery and Intervention Services(CRIS)

Collegiate Recovery and Intervention Services offers counseling and treatment planning for alcohol and substance use disorders. They also offer community support for those in recovery. Sessions are approximately $15 though there is financial assistance available. CRIS is located at 1101 Jackson Avenue. To schedule an appointment, call the office number during their office hours.

Office Number: 205-348-2727

UA Psychology Clinic

The Psychology Clinic offers individual therapy and psychological evaluations conducted by graduate students and supervised by licensed clinical psychologists. Sessions are $5 to $30 for UA students based on financial needs. The Psychology Clinic is located at 200 Hackberry Lane (McMillan Building). To schedule an appointment, call the office number during their office hours.

Office Number: 205-348-5000
